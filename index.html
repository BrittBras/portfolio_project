Alittle about Me...


Hi, I'm Brittani Michelle Braswell Long, formerly Brittani Braswell. I'm 30 years old. I love traveling, playing dnd, pizza with pineapples, stand up comedy, cosplay and attending sci-fi/pop culture conventions. I'm newly married to a wonderful husband and brilliant software developer, Robert Long, also step mom to my mischievous yet loveable little Landon. I'm current a student in Eleven Fifty Academy's part time Web Development cohort. I moved from Atlantan to Indy 3 years ago for a Resident Director position at Marian University. Before Web Development, I pursued a career in education. I worked in Higher Education Administration positions for 10 yrs along side esteemed faculty and staff from University of Georgia, Ga Tech, Liberty University, and IUPUI to name a few. I loved Higher Education so much but I found there was a lack of women, specifically women of color contributing to the research fields of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. That is why I'm here. It is ultimately my goal to learn to program; become an example to other young black women that we belong in STEM!
